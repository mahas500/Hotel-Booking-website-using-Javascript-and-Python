ABOUT US

We provide the services of hotel room booking to our clients. A user can access through variety of rooms according to price, ratings etc. After client finalizes the room he/she get's an email on their personal email id about the booking confirmation. In case of any issue client can contact us. He will be provided with incident reference number on his email ID. OTP based authentication is also provided to client in case they forget password and new users can first register on the website they get their customer id on email then they proceed to login. A admin manages all the bookings made by client. Admin can add the room into the portal and delete room from it.
